    FEATURES

    COLOR RESTORATION

    IMAIGE uses AI-technology to figure out what a black-and-white photo would have looked like with colors. After you've added an old black-and-white photo and wait for 1 minute, you're ready to go!

    PROMPTS

    AUDIO EDITING
